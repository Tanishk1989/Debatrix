# Debatrix

> **Debate anyone. Sharpen your mind.**

Debatrix is an AI-powered debate platform that helps users improve their **critical thinking**, **communication**, and **persuasion** skills by debating AI opponents and historical personalities.

## 🚀 Features

### 🤖 AI Debate

Challenge intelligent AI opponents on topics like:

* AI & Technology
* Geopolitics
* Economics
* Philosophy
* Psychology
* Society

The AI:

* Challenges weak arguments
* Gives counterarguments
* Uses evidence
* Remembers debate context
* Adapts difficulty

---

### ⚔️ Debate Modes

* **Classic** – User vs AI
* **Devil's Advocate** – AI always disagrees
* **Friendly Mentor** – Debate while learning
* **Hardcore** – AI aggressively attacks weak logic
* **Historical Mode** – Debate famous personalities

---

### 🎭 Historical Opponents

Debate legendary thinkers and leaders:

* Steve Jobs
* Socrates
* Albert Einstein
* Winston Churchill
* Napoleon Bonaparte
* Mahatma Gandhi
* Friedrich Nietzsche

Each opponent has:

* Unique personality
* Distinct speaking style
* Difficulty rating
* Historical viewpoints

---

### 🎤 Voice Debate

Speak naturally.

Debatrix can:

* Convert speech to text
* Respond in real-time
* Track speaking habits
* Analyze confidence and fluency

---

### 📊 Debate Evaluation

After each debate, Debatrix scores you on:

* Logic
* Evidence
* Clarity
* Persuasion
* Confidence

Get:

* Strengths
* Weaknesses
* Personalized feedback
* Suggestions for improvement

---

### 🏆 ELO Rating System

Inspired by Chess.com.

Track:

* Overall Rating
* Logic Rating
* Persuasion Rating
* Evidence Rating
* Confidence Rating

Climb the leaderboard as your debating skills improve.

---

### 🔥 Streaks & Achievements

Stay consistent with:

* Daily streaks
* XP and achievements
* Debate milestones
* Personalized challenges

Achievements include:

* First Victory
* Logic Master
* Persuasion Expert
* Beat Steve Jobs
* 30 Day Streak
